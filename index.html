OSULOTH Consulting Limited (OCL)

OSULOTH consulting Limited (OCL), is a local audit, tax and financial advisory firm that was incorporated, registered and authorized to operate in Uganda. It boasts of young, energetic, fully qualified certified accountants (ACCA and CPA) with local and regional professional experience in various business sectors and organizations.

Our firm is here to advise you on a wide range of issues from audit and assurance, tax, risk advisory, IFRS, consulting, transaction and business and financial advisory solutions. As well as these core services, our partner firms also offer a wide range of specialist services, such as wealth management, IT consulting, legal advisory, forensic accounting and human resource consulting.

The associates cover a full range of disciplines and include economists, engineers, agriculturalists, lawyers, medical doctors, human resource specialists, sociologists, and corporate governance specialists. From this wide range of experts, Osuloth can assemble any type of team required to carry out consultancy and advisory services.

So, rest assured wherever you are in the world and whatever your accounting and business advisory requirements, the OCL will collaborate to deliver solutions in a timely and professional manner.

OCL is committed to becoming one of the leading providers of professional audit and advisory services in Uganda. With the new developments regarding financial reporting where by most countries are adopting the International Financial Reporting Standards, the coming of Osuloth could never be timely.

Osuloth is committed to the economic and social transformation of Uganda. We will strive to develop a reputation and vision of providing high quality services to our clients.
